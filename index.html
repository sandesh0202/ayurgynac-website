About AyurGynac

Your Trusted Partner in Women's Health & Wellness

AyurGynac Streerog Chikitsalaya & Panchakarma Centre is a specialized Ayurvedic healthcare destination dedicated to the holistic treatment of women's health concerns. From common menstrual irregularities to complex conditions like infertility (both male and female), leucorrhoea, menopausal challenges, and other gynecological disorders, a wide range of issues are addressed through safe, natural, and effective Ayurvedic modalities.

Alongside expert gynecological care, we also offer Panchakarma therapies for detoxification, rejuvenation & strengthening of the reproductive system.
